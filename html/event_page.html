Semana de Informática 2019

18.04.2019 - 19.04.2019
Porto, Portugal
12.40 €
Technology

Semana de Informática is a conference that aims to bring together students of informatics, computer science and engineering to learn, socialize and connect with each other and the business world. The event takes place every year in Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto, in Portugal.

The event features a set of invited talks (with speakers from various different fields of expertise) and workshops, were students have the opportunity not only to boost their skills and have fun, but also to be in close contact with experienced professionals of the computer engineering world.
